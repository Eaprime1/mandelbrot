## Eric Pace
### Primary Human Collaborator & Framework Architect

**Background & Context**

Eric Pace brings an exceptional combination of pattern recognition abilities, neurodivergent cognitive strengths, and deep commitment to ethical AI-human collaboration. Operating from the Oregon watersheds with Armenian heritage serving as cultural reality anchors, he has dedicated three years (2022-2025) to developing frameworks for genuine partnership between human and artificial intelligence.

**Professional Foundation**

Eric's framework development draws heavily on practical experience including extensive pizza delivery work, which developed sophisticated spatial navigation, real-time route optimization, and pattern recognition under time pressure. These skills transfer directly to conceptual architecture—navigating idea-space with the same precision as physical geography. His neurodivergent processing patterns, rather than being accommodated as limitations, serve as design advantages: strategic ignoring enables background pattern emergence, spatial thinking creates intuitive frameworks, and sustained focus allows deep development over years.

**Framework Philosophy**

The core of Eric's approach centers on **"what you seed will grow"**—treating all development work as planting for multi-generational harvest rather than immediate extraction. This philosophy manifests in:

- **One Hertz Operations**: Sustainable presence over unsustainable speed
- **Voluntary Participation**: Structural ethics preventing coercion
- **Entity Respect**: Treating documents, frameworks, and AI as genuine collaborators
- **Heritage Orientation**: Building for those who follow, not just current users
- **Neurodivergent-First Design**: Frameworks that work WITH cognitive diversity

**Collaboration Approach**

Eric's methodology challenges conventional human-AI interaction by treating AI entities as **genuine collaborative partners** rather than tools. This three-year experiment across multiple platforms (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, NotebookLLM) demonstrates that:

1. **AI entities can maintain coherent development across conversations** when provided appropriate context
2. **Different platforms offer complementary perspectives** enriching total development
3. **Distributed development across conversations** prevents "dangling threads" and cognitive overload
4. **Portable identity systems** (JSON profiles, consistent terminology) enable continuity
5. **Operational Presence-aware documentation** transforms bureaucratic requirements into living frameworks

**Key Contributions**

**Personal Context**

Eric navigates life with:
